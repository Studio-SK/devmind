# Common Behavioral Questions — Mid-Level Backend

Grouped by theme. Each question includes a one-line note on what the interviewer
is actually probing for — use this to steer your STAR answer, not just answer
the literal question.

See `stories/` for the actual STAR-format answers built from your projects.

---

## Ownership & Initiative

- **Tell me about a time you took ownership of something beyond your assigned scope.**
  Looking for: do you wait to be told, or do you notice gaps and act.
- **Describe a project you drove end-to-end with minimal supervision.**
  Looking for: can you be trusted with ambiguity and still deliver.
- **Tell me about a time you identified a problem no one had asked you to solve.**
  Looking for: proactive judgment — do you see beyond your ticket.
- **Give an example of when you went above and beyond your role.**
  Looking for: discretionary effort, but watch for over-claiming — needs to sound proportionate, not heroic.
- **Tell me about a time you had to make a decision without complete information.**
  Looking for: comfort with ambiguity and a reasoned process, not just a guess that worked out.

## Conflict & Disagreement

- **Tell me about a time you disagreed with a teammate or manager on a technical decision.**
  Looking for: can you disagree respectfully and argue from evidence, not ego.
- **Describe a situation where you had to push back on a requirement or deadline.**
  Looking for: do you protect quality/scope with data, or just comply/complain.
- **Tell me about a time you had to work with someone difficult.**
  Looking for: emotional maturity — do you adapt your approach or just tolerate friction.
- **Have you ever been overruled on a decision? How did you handle it?**
  Looking for: can you commit to a team decision even after losing the argument.
- **Tell me about a conflict that didn't get resolved well. What would you do differently?**
  Looking for: self-awareness and honest reflection, not a rehearsed "and it worked out perfectly" story.

## Failure & Learning

- **Tell me about a time you made a significant mistake. What happened?**
  Looking for: honesty and accountability — do you own it or deflect blame.
- **Describe a project that failed or didn't go as planned.**
  Looking for: how you diagnose failure and what changed in your process afterward.
- **Tell me about a bug you shipped to production. How did you handle it?**
  Looking for: incident response instincts — detection, communication, fix, prevention.
- **What's something you learned the hard way?**
  Looking for: depth of reflection, not just "I learned to be more careful."
- **Tell me about a time you had to admit you were wrong.**
  Looking for: ego check — can you update your position when shown evidence.

## Growth & Feedback

- **Tell me about the toughest feedback you've received. How did you respond?**
  Looking for: do you get defensive or actually change behavior.
- **How do you seek out feedback proactively?**
  Looking for: whether growth is something that happens to you or something you pursue.
- **Tell me about a skill you deliberately worked on improving.**
  Looking for: self-directed learning, not just what a course/job forced on you.
- **Describe a time you gave difficult feedback to a peer.**
  Looking for: can you deliver critical feedback constructively, not just receive it.
- **Where do you want to grow in the next year, and what are you doing about it?**
  Looking for: whether your goals are concrete and whether you already have a plan.

## Collaboration & Teamwork

- **Tell me about a time you helped a struggling teammate.**
  Looking for: do you see team success as your success, not just your own tickets.
- **Describe working with a cross-functional team (PM, design, other eng teams).**
  Looking for: can you translate between technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- **Tell me about a time you had to influence someone without formal authority.**
  Looking for: persuasion through reasoning/trust-building, not just hierarchy.
- **How do you handle disagreements about code quality or style in reviews?**
  Looking for: whether reviews are collaborative or a battleground for you.
- **Tell me about a time communication broke down on a project. What did you do?**
  Looking for: do you fix process gaps or just individually route around them.

## Technical Decisions & Tradeoffs

- **Walk me through a significant technical decision you made and its tradeoffs.**
  Looking for: structured tradeoff reasoning — did you consider alternatives, or default to the familiar.
- **Tell me about a time you chose a simpler solution over a more "correct" one.**
  Looking for: pragmatism — do you over-engineer, or right-size to actual requirements.
- **Describe a time you had to balance speed vs. quality under a deadline.**
  Looking for: how you negotiate scope/tech debt consciously rather than accidentally.
- **Tell me about a technical decision you'd make differently now.**
  Looking for: growth over time — do you still think your old decisions were perfect.
- **How do you decide when to introduce a new technology/library vs. use what's already in the stack?**
  Looking for: whether you evaluate cost of adoption (maintenance, team familiarity) or chase novelty.
